Save Dat Monie is a Chrome extension that helps you save money and control your shopping addiction. Every time you go to payment page, you will be alerted with a reminder of achieving your goal.



If you can read this and see the little dollar sign next to your address bar in chrome, it means that Save Dat Monie is already active on your browser. Go ahead and ignore this popup from now on!
